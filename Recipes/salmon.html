Baked salmon

Description

This is a basic baked salmon recipe,Enjoy

  • salmon fillet
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • one clove of garlic
  • Fresh herb mix
  • pinch of parsley
  • 1 lemon sliced
  • pinch of thyme

Steps

  1. preheat salmon at 400 degerees
  2. In a bowl stir olive oil, garlic,herbs, and lemon juice.
  3. Spoon over salon and bake for 12-15 minutes
  4. garnish with thyme, parsley and lemon slices
