Potato Wedges

Description

This is a fast and easy recipe for potato wedges, Enjoy!

  • 6 good sized potatoes peeled
  • Half cup of olive oil
  • Tablespoon of salt, pepper
  • Dash of garlic poweder
  1. Preheat over to 450 degrees
  2. cut potatoes into wedges and pat dry with paper towel
    3. device-width
  3. mix oil and garlic, salt and peper in a bowl
  4. Toss potatoes in mixture and place on parchment paper
  5. Cook for 55 minutes
